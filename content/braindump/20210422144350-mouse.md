emacs -nw에서 mouse 사용  


## config {#config}

```elisp
;; <<use-package::mouse>>
(use-package! mouse
  :unless window-system
  :bind
  ("<mouse-4>" . scroll-down-line)
  ("<mouse-5>" . scroll-up-line)
  :config
  ;; (setq mouse-wheel-tilt-scroll t)
  (xterm-mouse-mode 1)
  (setq track-mouse nil)
  ;; (defun track-mouse (e))
  (setq mouse-sel-mode t)
  )
```
