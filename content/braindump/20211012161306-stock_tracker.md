## config {#config}

```elisp
(use-package! stock-tracker
  :config
  ;; Monitor list of stocks
  (setq stock-tracker-list-of-stocks
        '("0601595" "0601857"))
  ;; Refresh stock price every 5*10 secs
  (setq stock-tracker-refresh-interval 5)
  ;; Example Key binding
  (global-set-key (kbd "C-c s") 'stock-tracker-start))
```
